How to Use It

  1. Open the Music Library page.
  2. Pick a category (Warm-Up, Timeouts, Intros).
  3. Queue songs in your software (DJay Pro, Soundboard, Spotify).
  4. Test volume & transitions before doors open.

Pro tip: add short stingers between whistle and ball-in to avoid dead air.
Keep backups ready in case of timeouts changing length.

Quick Glossary

Stinger
A 3–8 second music hit used to punctuate a PA line or moment.
Walk-in
Music played while fans enter; medium energy, familiar hooks.
Bumper
Short clip used to cover transitions between reads or replays.
